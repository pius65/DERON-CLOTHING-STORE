About Deron Clothing

Deron Clothing is Nairobi's premier denim destination, offering high-quality, affordable denim wear since 2023. We specialize in:

  • Trendy denim fashion for men and women
  • Ethically sourced materials
  • Custom fittings and alterations
  • Exceptional customer service

Our mission is to provide stylish, comfortable denim that fits your lifestyle and budget.

Return & Exchange Policy

48-Hour Return Policy

We accept returns and exchanges within 48 hours of purchase under these conditions:

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Original tags must still be attached
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility

Exchange Process

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of purchase
  2. Return the item to our store
  3. Choose replacement item or store credit

Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed within 1-3 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
